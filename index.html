My story

I'm an experienced journalist who is now a UX Designer. With print investing in mobile technologies and the web, I decided to make the switch to stay ahead of the curve and evolve as a professional.

Journalism = UX

Believe it not, they are the same. Researching, interviewing, workflows, wireframing, layout, typography, copywriting — I did all of this on a day-to-day basis as a reporter, editor and layout/page designer.

Mobile platforms

I love apps and all forms of mobile technology. I've owned Droids, Blackberries (yep) and iPhones. Mobile is the future. Who knows what that will exactly look like, but that's what's exciting.
